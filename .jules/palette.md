## 2026-02-25 - Icon Button Accessibility
**Learning:** Interactive icon-only buttons in the frontend require explicit `aria-label` attributes and `focus-visible` classes (specifically `focus-visible:ring-2` and `focus-visible:ring-maya-500`) to ensure accessibility for screen reader and keyboard users.
**Action:** Always check and add `aria-label` and `focus-visible` styles when touching icon-only buttons.
